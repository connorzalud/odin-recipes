Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies and chewy, soft and delicious. The dough can be left in the fridge overnight to develop its flavor further, or the prepared dough can be frozen and baked at any time.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips/Chunks

Steps

  1. Melt butter and mix in the sugars and salt.
  2. Whisk in egg and vanilla extract.
  3. Sift in flour and baking powder. Fold together until just combined.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips.
  5. Chill dough if desired.
  6. Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes, until edges are just golden brown.
  7. Allow to cool.
